Our Journey

September 2024

Project initiated with the first beach cleanup event in Kiryandongo.

November 2024

Established first collection points and recruited initial volunteers.

January 2025

Launched educational programs and community awareness campaigns.

March 2025

Expanded to neighboring communities and established partnerships.

May 2025

Celebrating our achievements and planning future expansion.

Voices of Change

Ajak Deng Chol

Community Coordinator | Community Outreach Director

"Our health initiatives have made significant strides in providing accessible healthcare to refugee families. We've seen dramatic improvements in both physical and mental well-being through our comprehensive support programs."

Dr. Nia Thompson

Healthcare Program Coordinator

"The transformation in our community has been incredible. By bringing together diverse voices and experiences, we've created a support network that empowers refugee families to thrive and contribute to their new home."

Mr. Jamal Patel

Community Integration Specialist
